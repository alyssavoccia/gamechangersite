ATHLETE RESOURCES provided by NCPH

Game Changer is a confidential and free service

Everyone faces stress at some point in life. Student athletes uniquely balance sport and acamdemics Sometimes, maintaining this constant balance can be stressful. The stress can negatively impact athletic performance, grades, social relationships and overall happiness. Normal life challenges can quickly become overwhelming. We help you to be more successful in every area of your college life.

Solutions are individually tailored.

Support is just a phone call away

EASY TO ACCESS - Simply call us to speak with a profesional counselor.

WE ARE AVAILABLE - 24 hours a day (for emergencies), 7 days a week.

FREE - Limestone College Athletics cares about you. There are NO out of pocket expenses for unlimited phone calls and up to 5 face-to-face r telehealth sessions per year. This resource is provided as a student athlete benefit.

CONFIDENTIAL - All discussions are private and voluntary. Conversations, records of sessions and referrals are held in the utmost confidence, and can only be released with your signed consent.

BE OPTIMISTIC - We are here to support you, and together we will identify valuable resources to help you find comfort. Many times, sessions with a counselor can be helpful.
