COACHES RESOURCES provided by NCPH

Game Changer is a confidential and free service

Everyone faces stress at some point in life. We have to do more in less time. We have children, significant others and parents who require our attention. These normal life challenges can become overwhelming and not only affect our everyday lives, but also our job performance. We may forget to connect with our family, friends, and co-workers in healthy ways. Our relationships suffer and we feel like we are all alone. There is help.

Solutions are individually tailored.

Support is just a phone call away

EASY TO ACCESS - Recpetion is our single point of entry. Just call to speak with a professional counselor to see how can best meet your needs.

WE ARE AVAILABLE - 24 hours a day (for emergencies), 7 days a week.

FREE - There are NO out of pocket expenses for unlimited phone calls and up to 5 face-to-face session per year. Limestone College Athletics cares about you and your family. This resource is provided as an employee benefit.

CONFIDENTIAL - All discussions are private and voluntary. Conversations, records of sessions and referrals are held in the utmost confidence, and can only be released with your signed consent.

DON'T BE DISCOURAGED - We are here to support you, and together we will identify valuable resources to help you find comfort. Many times, sessions with a counselor can be helpful.
